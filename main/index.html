About LII

Likoma Institute of Innovation (LII) is Malawi’s future-facing academic hub dedicated to nurturing thinkers, makers, and doers. Located on Likoma Island, we blend tradition with technology to foster innovation that serves both local and global communities.

Technology & Entrepreneurship

Equipping students to develop scalable digital and physical solutions to real-world problems.

Marine & Environmental Sciences

Training leaders to preserve Lake Malawi's ecosystem through innovative, sustainable practices.

Leadership & Social Innovation

Empowering changemakers who drive impact through policy, education, and social design.
