About Likoma Institute of Innovation (LII)

Likoma Institute of Innovation (LII) is a premier academic institution located on the serene island of Likoma. Established with a bold vision to nurture African tech leaders, LII combines academic rigor with innovation and community impact.

We are committed to empowering students with future-ready skills in software engineering, data science, social innovation, and entrepreneurship. Our learning environment blends research, real-world application, and mentorship to equip graduates for global relevance.

Our Vision

To be a leading innovation hub in Africa, shaping transformative leaders who drive sustainable development through technology.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class education, foster research and innovation, and create solutions that uplift communities across the continent.
